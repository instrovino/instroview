Company Profile

INSTROVIEW is an Turnkey Project solution provider established in 2018. It is based in Chennai, having branch offices across India.

Instroview Specialized in Industrial TurnKey Project for Electrical and Instrumentation, which is based on Erection, Installation, Commissioning and Maintenance of Oil &s; Gas, Fertilizers, Petrochemical Industries service provider. Our business encompasses wide sectors of Process Automation, Factory Automation, Testing & Commissioning &s; Calibration of measuring Instruments

At INSTROVIEW, Our knowledge and expertise is tailored to maximise the lifespan and output of your production process, at the lowest possible cost to your organisation. INSTROVIEW focus is on Prevention, quality and productivity, leading to industry leading safety procedures, observance of contractual obligations and regulations. The quality with which we approach and perform on-site work is both innovative and accountable. Our maintenance contracts are carried out with the commitment to deliver results.

We have experience in a wide range of industrial maintenance services, including:

  1. Operation & Maintenance of Equipments
  2. Preventative and Reliability Maintenance
  3. Workshop Maintenance Overhauling of Control Valves
  4. Instrument & Measuring Device Calibration
  5. Turn Around / Shutdown Activites.

We have developed long-term partnership with system integrators, and value added seller in various fields. We are also engaged in Custom design solutions using off the self-available components to meet various stringent applications.

Instroview combines the skill and talent of its engineers with the technical expertise to provide solutions in the field of Communications, Test & Measurement, Automated solutions using low power consumption & compact design. Some of key people of Instroview having 15 to 20 years of Industrial experience.
